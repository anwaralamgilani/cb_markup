Blogging

An opportunity to talk to your customers directly

There are many tools that can help you search your leads but there is only one tool that help your leads to find you and that’s blogging.

We can turn blogging from isolated effort to a major marketing channel for your business. Blogging can pave path for your success.

How can you stand out in crowd through blogging?

 

Traffic Generation

Right business blogging makes sure you rank better in search engines. In fact, last year, businesses with active blogs saw more than 2.5 times increase in traffic. This traffic was generated from search engine, social media and other referral sites through blogging.

Lead Generation

Generating traffic is one thing and converting traffic is another. To convert traffic into leads we integrate CTAs and landing pages within your blog. According to recent research, marketers who have dynamic blogs generate 67% more leads.

Email Integration

Great content always attract readers. An email subscription service helps readers to keep in-touch with your blog. Your reader is notified via email every time you post a new entry on your blog.
 

How we help you achieve your blogging goals?

At Cranberry, we monitor your industry and generate content that can actually educate your readers and help them solve their problems using your product/service.

To make your blog easily accessible to your prospects, we optimize each blog entry for search engines, social media channels and mobile users. This optimization is not just about Meta Tags but it covers all aspects of on-page optimization.

There is no use of generating traffic if you can’t covert traffic into leads. We create, optimize and integrate landing pages within your blog posts to convert visitors into quality leads and future customers.

Without proper management anything can lead to the wrong path and all efforts can go in vain. We organize, schedule and then publish blog content. Content management help to coordinate the campaigns across the blog, landing pages, email marketing and social media.

Measuring each process is a factor which is often overlooked. We believe in measuring each step of our procedure to get better results for our clients. This also lead us to find what works and what doesn’t. Once everything has been done accurately, we celebrate with you.
