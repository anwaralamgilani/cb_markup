Our Story

Vision & Mission

Change is the only constant in the universe & everything around us, including us, is changing constantly – Humans work around their necessities & – necessity is the mother of invention. The evolution of telephone from Graham Bell to Martin Cooper (inventor of mobile phone) to iPhone – self explains the necessity and invention.

We too observed and learnt the same change from necessities to inventions in our living to our working, to our selection and to our buying. These changes need to be catered with evolved methods even in marketing, right?

Cranberry, conceptualized in 2007, adopted the evolved methodology, Inbound, for itself and opt-to help businesses to resolve their marketing with the same. This methodology evolved to attract, engage and delight the customers in inbound experience.

Cruel reality of current marketing situation

Say yes if you find it ridiculous that businesses still use cold calls, offers, invitations, spam emails or SMS for marketing in this era. And you also agree that these are the most irritating marketing acts? Hurray! We are on same page.

Which business doesn’t want increase in number of visitors? Which salesman doesn’t want to have more leads to convert? Which business manager doesn’t want to have more customers to be closed? Absolutely, everybody is in the market for these goals. Say YES if you agree with us.!

Inbound is the solution

If we disapprove irritating marketing acts, then what are we suppose to do? There must be a transformation in the ways marketing can be performed. The need of the hour is to derive tactics that can attract, convert and close customers. And the solution lies in two words,
“Inbound Marketing”.

Cranberry achieved success by transforming its business from irritating to Inbound. And now we have one simple goal: help businesses to get back into the game.

How we achieved it?

Cranberry was conceptualized in 2007 with the aim to excel in PR industry. We focused to build a culture within the organization and backed it up with an ambitious and enthusiastic “team”. Every member of our team is passionate, crazy and talented enough to transform how organizations market and sell.

With the same passion and talent, we aim to make your marketing better so that people start believing in you again!
