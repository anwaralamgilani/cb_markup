Inbound Marketing?

Inbound & Marketing Elements

Different marketing elements are utilized at different levels of marketing funnel to define the journey of a prospect. The purpose of each marketing element at each level of the funnel is to throw the prospect to the next level. A prospect starts the journey as a stranger and converted to a user, then a user is converted into the lead, lead is then converted to a customer and at last the customer is converted to a promoter of your product/service. Cranberry offers full support to you through out your Inbound marketing execution.
